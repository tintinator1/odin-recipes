Chicken Tacos

This chicken tacos recipe is perfect for those nights when you don't have a lot of time for dinner preparations.
Feel free to add chili powder or paprika to the chicken if your family likes it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 2 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lemonade, olive oil, lime juice, and Worcestershire sauce in a large skillet over medium heat.
    Stir in garlic powder, onion powder, and bay leaf.
    Simmer until chicken is tender and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.

  2. Meanwhile, warm the tortillas in the oven or microwave until soft.
    When chicken is fully cooked, transfer to serving bowl.
    Place chopped tomatoes, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream in serving dishes.
    Each person can create their own wrap, using their preferred ingredients.
