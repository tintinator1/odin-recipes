Fried Rice

A quick fried rice recipe like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant.
Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need.
Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients
  2. Place carrots in small saucepan and cover with water.
    Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
    Stir in peas, then drain in colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds.
    Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat.
    Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
